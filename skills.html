Skills & Tools

Technical depth, policy fluency

I work at the intersection of quantitative analysis and institutional governance. Below is how I think about my capabilities — not by tool name, but by what each layer of skill makes possible.

Data Analysis & Querying

Turning raw data into structured findings

The foundation of every project. I work with large, messy, multi-source datasets and build the SQL and Python pipelines that make reliable analysis possible. Applied across EU research projects, personal analytics projects, and professional client work.

SQL (BigQuery) CTEs & Window Functions Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib) R (ggplot2, statistical testing) Advanced Excel Google Sheets

Day-to-day working tools. Applied in EU research projects, CLV analysis, cohort analysis, and A/B testing work.

Statistical Modelling & Forecasting

Building models that hold up under scrutiny

Forecasting, scenario modelling, and rigorous hypothesis testing. I apply econometric methods to policy questions and statistical methods to product and business questions — and I document assumptions explicitly so that models can be owned and updated by the teams that use them.

Time Series Analysis (ARIMA, Exponential Smoothing) Regression Analysis Hypothesis Testing Confidence Intervals A/B Testing & Power Analysis Scenario Modelling Partial-Equilibrium Modelling MAPE & Variance Analysis

Applied in EU Just Transition policy modelling (EESC, 2026), Udacity A/B testing analysis, and subscription cohort work.

Business Intelligence & Visualisation

Making findings impossible to ignore

Analysis that cannot be communicated clearly has limited institutional value. I build executive dashboards and analytical visualisations designed for non-technical decision-makers — from EU institution reports to C-suite dashboards — with a focus on making the insight visible without requiring the reader to do interpretive work.

Power BI (Executive Dashboards) DAX Tableau KPI Framework Design Data Storytelling EU Institutional Report Design

Delivered dashboards for Citibank C-suite leadership, HR analytics tools, and published EU research outputs for the European Parliament and EESC.

Policy Design & Governance Frameworks

Connecting evidence to institutional decision-making

The layer most data portfolios omit. I design governance frameworks, conduct regulatory gap analysis, and produce policy briefs that move from data to recommendation to action. I have done this inside Kenyan government ministries, EU-funded research teams, and African development organisations — and I understand that what works institutionally in Brussels and what works in Nairobi are not the same thing.

Governance Framework Design Regulatory Gap Analysis Policy Brief Writing KPI & Indicator Design Key Informant Interviews Stakeholder Consultation Mixed Methods Research M&E Framework Design

Applied at Ministry of Health Kenya, EU research projects (EP, EESC, DG CNECT), FSD Africa, and Visionary Analytics.

AI Ethics & Digital Governance

Responsible AI, grounded in real governance contexts

AI Fairness & Ethics certified (Turing College, EQF Level 7, 2024). I apply ethical frameworks and algorithmic accountability principles to real governance questions — particularly in African and European contexts where the institutional conditions, power asymmetries, and data infrastructure constraints are materially different. I am committed to AI governance systems that are not just technically sound but contextually appropriate.

Bias Detection in ML/AI Systems Algorithmic Accountability Frameworks Fairness Metrics Digital Sovereignty Platform Economy Governance AI Policy Design EU Digital Services Act

Applied in Turing College certification coursework and directly relevant to EU Digital Services Act research at Visionary Analytics and the European Software & Cyber Dependencies report for the European Parliament.
